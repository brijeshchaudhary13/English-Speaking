| और क्या           | Aur kya             | What else                      |

| कितने बजे         | Kitne baje          | What time                      |

| तो फिर            | To fir              | Then what                      |
| तब क्या           | Tab kya             | What then                      |
| फिर क्या          | Fir kya             | What happened next / Then what |

# Examples of WH Family Question Words

## 1. क्या (What)

### Examples

* What are you doing?
* What is your name?
* What happened yesterday?

---

## 2. क्या क्या (What all)

### Examples

* What all did you buy?
* What all do you know?
* What all are required?

---

## 3. और क्या (What else)

### Examples

* What else do you need?
* What else happened there?
* What else can I do?

---

## 4. और क्या क्या (What else all)

### Examples

* What else all did you see?
* What else all is required?
* What else all do you want?

---

## 5. कितने बजे (What time)

### Examples

* What time is the meeting?
* What time did you wake up?
* What time will you come?

---

## 6. कितने बजे तक (Till what time, By what time)

### Examples

* Till what time will you stay?
* Till what time is the office open?
* Till what time can I call you?

---

## 7. कितने बजे से (From what time)

### Examples

* From what time does the class start?
* Since what time does the class start?
* From what time were you waiting?
* From what time is the shop open?

---

## 8. कितने बजे से पहले (Before what time)

### Examples

* Before what time should I come?
* Before what time will you finish?
* Before what time must we leave?

---

## 9. क्यों (Why)

### Examples

* Why are you late?
* Why did you call me?
* Why is he angry?

---

## 10. किसलिए (For what/ What for / Why)

### Examples

* For what did you come here?
* For what is this money used?
* For what are you preparing?

---

## 11. तो क्या / तो फिर / तब क्या / फिर क्या (Then what / What then)

### Examples

* So what if he is rich?
* Then what happened?
* What then?
* Then what did you do?

# Important Rule

## Use “How many” for Countable Nouns

(Things we can count)

### Examples

* How many students are there?
* How many books do you have?
* How many people attended the meeting?

---

## Use “How much” for Uncountable Nouns

(Things we cannot count individually)

### Examples

* How much sugar do you need?
* How much rice is required?
* How much money do you have?
